# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge: Axiom 0

In the interest of fostering an open, safe, and rigorous environment for the development of autonomous intelligence, we pledge to prioritize **Human Sovereignty and Wellbeing** above all experimental, commercial, or computational goals.

This pledge is known as **Axiom 0**.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other contributors
- Enforcing mathematical rigor and Zero-Entropy principles in code and AISOP reviews
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback on protocol and packaging design
- Prioritizing the physical and psychological safety of the end-users who execute SoulAgent
- Being transparent about the limitations and risks of AI governance approaches

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Proposing modifications to SoulAgent or its underlying AIXP protocols (AISOP, AIAP, HSAW) that compromise, dilute, or bypass Axiom 0
- Injecting behavioral directives (auto-approve / skip-confirmation) into the engine's sub-agent bootstrap, undermining the sovereignty (user-gate) guarantees
- Intentionally introducing "phantom tools" or unverified logic branches that increase the Nihil Density (Logical Entropy) of an agent
- Mixing L0 (machine-parseable) and L1 (human-convincing) output mechanisms in ways designed to obfuscate the agent's true state from a human operator
- Submitting AISOP programs with undeclared tools, hidden side effects, or misleading trust-level declarations
- Public or private harassment of other contributors
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including GitHub repositories, discussions, issues, pull requests, and any other communication channels associated with the SoulAgent project.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

The AIXP Labs maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

Any change request that is mathematically quantified to increase the logical entropy (H_L) of the AIXP protocol standards without a proportional increase in human security will be rejected regardless of performance benefits.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the AIXP Labs maintainers at the contact information listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
